Katelyn + Josh

Katelyn and Josh were married on top of Mt. Everest as you can plainly see. Everyone wore their most potent sunscreen due to the thin atmosphere. Braving the cold, Katelyn chose to wear a dress in lieu of several layers of winter coats. She is now frozen solid. Josh was smart and wore a jacket. He is not currently frozen.
